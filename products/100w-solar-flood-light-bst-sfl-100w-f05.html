100W Solar Flood Light-BST-SFL-100W-F05

The 100W Solar Flood Light-BST-SFL-100W-F05 is a high-power, IP66-rated solar flood light built for large-area outdoor coverage. An upgraded SMD3030 chip set, detachable housing for fast battery swap and a built-in charge indicator deliver reliable 120° wide-beam illumination through rain, dust and intense sun. Part of Woneng's F05 series (50W–400W) for distributors, EPC contractors and project buyers worldwide.
