All In One Solar Street Light BST-AIO-2520P-S04

High-output all-in-one solar street light, S04 series.

The All In One Solar Street Light BST-AIO-2520P-S04 is a high-output all-in-one solar street light with built-in SMD3030 chips and a brand-new LiFePO4 battery. Its corrosion-resistant housing offers excellent heat dissipation and IP66 waterproofing, delivering stable all-night performance for roads, parks and industrial sites.
