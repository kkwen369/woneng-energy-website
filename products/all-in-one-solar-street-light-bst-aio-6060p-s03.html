All In One Solar Street Light BST-AIO-6060P-S03

All-in-one solar street light, S03 series.

The All In One Solar Street Light BST-AIO-6060P-S03 is an all-in-one solar street light integrating lamp, LiFePO4 battery, solar panel and smart controller in a single corrosion-resistant unit. A built-in new LiFePO4 battery (2000+ charge cycles), high-brightness 180 lm/W LED chips and intelligent brightness control make it a low-maintenance choice for municipal and rural roads, backed by a 3–5 year warranty.
