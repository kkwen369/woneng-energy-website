The Dimond Series Solar Wall Light -BST-SWL-21 (Dimond Series) is a solar wall light with 32 LED beads for wide 180° lighting angles and high efficiency. Built-in lithium battery (up to 5-year life), PIR motion sensing (120°, 5–7 m) and IP65 protection suit residential and commercial façades.
