Municipal LED Street Light BST-STL-150W-B

Municipal AC LED street light, STL series.

The Municipal LED Street Light BST-STL-150W-B is a municipal-grade AC LED street light with two installation options for easy setup. IP65 waterproof housing resists heavy rain and storms, die-cast aluminium ensures fast heat dissipation, and an isolated / brand power supply is optional for public roads, highways and urban projects.
