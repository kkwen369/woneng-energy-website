Solar Post Light BST-ZTD-003

Solar post light, ZTD series.

The Solar Post Light BST-ZTD-003 is a solar post light with a die-cast ABS + PC body and Class A solar panel. Compact and corrosion-resistant, it adds safe, automatic dusk-to-dawn lighting to fences, decks, pathways and garden posts (IPX4 / IP65 by model).
