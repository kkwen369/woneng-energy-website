Lens Integrated Solar Wall Light BST-SWL-19/20

Lens-integrated solar wall light, SWL series.

The Lens Integrated Solar Wall Light BST-SWL-19/20 is a lens-integrated solar wall light with a built-in lithium battery (up to 5-year life) and multiple lighting modes. PIR motion sensing and IP65 protection make it suited to building façades, courtyards, driveways and perimeter walls.
